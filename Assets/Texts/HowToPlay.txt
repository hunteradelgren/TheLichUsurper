How To Play:
Melee Attacks are stronger and are performed with the Left Mouse Button
Ranged attacks offer safety to attack from a distance but are weaker, they are performed with the right mouse Button
After you clear a room be sure to pick up you rewards. There are four types of pickups:
	Health restore will restore you health, but it wont restore your spirit
	Health upgrade will increase you max health along with restoring some health
	Melee damage upgrade increases your sword damage by one point
	Spell power upgrade strengthens your spells by one point
